Our Vision

To build creative, disciplined and dynamic leaders of tomorrow who will lead others by example and be active global citizens contributing richly to both our Nation and the World.

Our Mission

To offer a dynamic educational program to students of diverse backgrounds that encourages each student to achieve his/her highest potential; to facilitate an inquiry based approach that will make them life long learners; to offer a unique blend of curriculum that enables easy transition from school to tertiary education anywhere in the world; to practice the ethics of love and compassion towards all and commit themselves in creating a peaceful world.

Our Motto

Empowering every child.
